Information On Taurus Astrology

The Taurus Zodiac Sign: Complete Taurus Horoscope

Element: Earth

Color: Green, Pink

Quality: Fixed

Day: Friday, Monday

Ruler: Venus

Greatest Compatibility: Scorpio , Cancer

Lucky Numbers: 2, 6, 9, 12, 24

Dates: April 20 - May 20

Taurus Personality Traits

Strengths: Reliable, patient, practical, devoted, responsible, stable

Weaknesses: Stubborn, possessive, uncompromising

Taurus likes: Gardening, cooking, music, romance, high quality clothes, working with hands

Taurus dislikes: Sudden changes, complications, insecurity of any kind, synthetic fabrics

By: Our Astrologers

Taurus is a practical and well-grounded zodiac sign that values the fruits of their labor. They have a strong desire to be surrounded by love and beauty, and are drawn to the material world and physical pleasures. Individuals born under this sign are sensual and tactile, placing great importance on touch and taste as their primary senses. They are also known for being stable and conservative, making them one of the most dependable signs of the zodiac. Taurus individuals are committed to their choices and will endure until they reach a point of personal satisfaction.

Taurus is an Earth sign , Like Virgo and Capricorn . Taurus has a grounded, practical, and realistic perspective. They have a talent for making money and sticking with long-term projects until completion. Their determination and commitment are often mistaken for stubbornness, but they simply have a strong sense of responsibility. Taurus makes great employees, long-term friends, and partners, as they are always there for the people they love. However, their earthly nature can also make them overprotective, conservative, or materialistic at times, as their views of the world are influenced their desire for financial stability.

Venus is the ruling planet of Taurus, which influences their love of beauty, attraction, satisfaction, creativity, and gratitude. This tender nature makes them great cooks, gardeners, lovers, and artists. Taurus individuals are loyal and don't like sudden changes, criticism, or feeling guilty. They are somewhat dependent on other people and their emotions, and may have difficulty letting go of certain feelings. Despite any emotional challenges, Taurus individuals have a gift for providing a practical voice of reason in chaotic and unhealthy situations.

Taurus – the Wandering Bull in mythology, Taurus betrayed their best friend, goddess Hera, and was condemned to wander the earth in search of freedom. Taurus often feels a sense of unease, as if something is constantly reminding them of past happiness, and they may retreat into their own world, feeling lonely and disconnected from their true selves. To find love and happiness, Taurus individuals need to travel the world, shift their perspective, or make adjustments to their belief system and values.

Taurus Love, Sex &Compatibility

Patience is key when it comes to being in a relationship with a Taurus. They are extremely sensual and value touch, smell, and all pleasurable senses, but they also need time to create a safe environment and relax during sexual encounters. When they develop enough intimacy with a loved one, they may become a bit clingy and needy, but they still try to keep their emotions in check by relying on practical reasoning while embracing change and their partner's initiatives.

Taurus individuals typically choose long-term partners from their social environment who can meet their intellectual needs and the expectations of their family and close friends. They hold onto traditional values and the practical aspects of life, so they rarely choose partners who don't meet the basic expectations of their upbringing. Taurus individuals often show and receive attention through gifts and material things. However, if they stick too rigidly to their moral code and refuse to be adventurous or explore taboos, they may become frustrated and struggle with anger issues that they don't know how to resolve, which may manifest in their relationships. Compatibility with other zodiac signs can be complicated for Taurus.

Taurus Friends and Family

Friends - Taurus individuals are loyal and always willing to extend a hand of friendship, although they may initially be closed off to the outer world until they build trust with new social contacts. Many of their friendships begin in childhood and tend to last a lifetime. Once they establish a clear, intimate connection with another person, they will do everything in their power to nurture the relationship and make it functional, even in difficult times.

Family – Home and family are of utmost importance to Taurus individuals. They love spending time with loved ones, including children, and respect family routines, customs, and traditions. They enjoy hosting gatherings for both family and friends, and don't mind cooking a meal for a room full of people if everyone is having fun. Taurus individuals cherish family events and are present for every occasion.

Taurus Career and Money

Taurus individuals are known for their love of money and their willingness to work hard to earn it. They are reliable, hardworking, patient, and thorough in both employee and leadership roles. Once they focus on a specific project, they will firmly stick to it, regardless of external distractions. Stability is a key factor in their work routine. They seek material pleasures and rewards to build their own sense of value and achieve a luxurious yet practical way of life. For Taurus individuals, their job is a means to this end.

Taurus is a well-organized zodiac sign when it comes to finances, and they prioritize paying all of their bills on time. They take responsibility for their pension and save money for a rainy day. They can make do with a small or large salary equally well. Suitable occupations for Taurus individuals include agriculture, banking, art, and any career that requires culinary skills.

Taurus Man: A Lover's Guide

If you're looking for a strong, loyal, and generous man, a Taurus is an excellent choice. He is trustworthy, patient, and tender in love, always seeking a mutual emotional connection. He is not one to pick up on subtle hints and suggestive looks from those who flirt with him and may be a bit slow to respond, as if waiting for someone to make the first move. He dislikes artificiality of any kind and values genuine conversations, especially when it comes to compliments and declarations of love.

A Taurus man needs time to build trust, and anyone seeking his heart should be prepared to take the time to earn it. As a man of few words, he may seem impenetrable at times, as if nothing can touch him. When dating a Taurus man, a delicious home-cooked meal is always a safe bet, as well as choosing a comfortable and cozy place rather than a popular or trendy spot. He is turned to nature and common sense, and sees sex as something that should happen when the time is right. He rarely puts pressure on his partner and enjoys it as an experience to be savored rather than craved.

One aspect of his fixed and static character is the potential inability to forgive betrayal. He needs to feel truly safe and secure to settle down with one partner for the long term.

Taurus Woman: A Lover's Guide

To seduce a Taurus woman, it's important to appeal to her sense of romance. Taurus women want to be courted and slowly seduced, even if they have already decided to enter a relationship with someone. They prefer things to move slowly and will rarely jump into a sexual bond quickly without thinking long and hard about their choices.

A Taurus woman desires true love and security. It's unlikely that she will give into her desires and instincts quickly and if someone wishes to win her heart, they will have to spend a lot of time and energy courting her and making her feel comfortable. Once she falls in love she becomes affectionate, intimate, close and loyal. Standing by her partner for as long as he is faithful to her. She has an eye for beautiful things and appreciates simplicity and fine things in life. The best way to approach her is through enjoyable shared moments, respect for her privacy, fine food, and a gentle touch. This woman doesn't like to feel rushed when dating and needs to have her time. Once she feels comfortable and secure with someone, she will quickly give her heart without holding back.
